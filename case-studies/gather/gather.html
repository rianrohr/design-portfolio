Gather: A Planner Virtual Assistant

Empowering community engagement

Summary

Gather is a prototype designed to enhance event discovery and scheduling by integrating AI and APIs. It personalizes recommendations, suggests local events, and syncs with user calendars.

  • Market research
  • API exploration
  • Competitive analysis
  • Wireframing
  • Low- to high-fidelity design

Problem

Finding relevant, local, and up-to-date events is frustrating and disjointed across current platforms.

Challenge

Existing tools lack personalization and smooth integration. The goal was to design a smart, intuitive interface offering tailored, actionable results.

Current Interface Comparison

Today’s platforms often provide generic suggestions with minimal geolocation and calendar functionality.

AI and API Research

I explored ChatGPT, Bard, and IBM Watson for AI. ChatGPT stood out for its NLP capabilities. APIs: Eventbrite (events), Google Maps (location), and Google Calendar (scheduling).

Low-Fidelity Sketches

Initial sketches outlined the app’s structure and interactions.

Interface Development

I moved from sketches to mid- and high-fidelity designs in Figma, refining flows with feedback.

Reflection and Conclusion

This project taught me how to combine technical tools with design empathy to solve real-world problems in a scalable way.
